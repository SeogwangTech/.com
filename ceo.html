Chairman's Greetings

Hello. We would like to express our sincere gratitude to everyone who visited Seogwang Tech.

Since its establishment, Seogwang Tech has grown as an eco-friendly chemical company based on customer trust in the textile chemical field for over 20 years. We have provided optimal solutions to domestic and international customers based on quality and technology that are competitive in the global market, beyond the domestic production of high-functional chemical materials.

Recently, the industrial environment is changing rapidly, and sustainability and eco-friendly management are becoming core values that are directly related to the survival of companies. In line with this, Seogwang Tech is strengthening its R&D capabilities and is aiming to take a leap forward through eco-friendly production processes and smart automation.

Beyond simply producing products, we consider our role as a 'partner' who thinks about and solves customers' problems together as the most important. Based on our customer-centered management philosophy, we will continue to create a better future through honest and transparent management and technological innovation.

We ask for your continued interest and support, and we will do our best to become a company that is always trusted and grows together with you. Thank you.

Seogwang Tech Chairman Lee Beom-so
